About Us

The Goal

At Patchwork Pharma, our goal is to distribute access to internally shelved R&D data (abandoned target behavior data, clinical trial data, pharmacovigilance data, etc.) and promote secured collaborations with novel forms of contributor attribution.

Data access and communication problems create a significant barrier to entry for pharmaceutical startups and slows their ability to scale, hampering the rate at which life-changing innovations can be developed.

As therapeutics become increasingly niche and individualized, it will be critical for market makers to be young, lean, and innovative in their approaches. Our mission is to facilitate a new era of decentralized R&D to accomplish this.
